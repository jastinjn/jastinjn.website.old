About Me

Hey there! My name is Justin Ng. I am currently a second year student studying Computer Engineering at the Univeristy of Michigan.

I was born in Singapore on July 18th, 1999. In early 2002, my family moved to Redwood City, California where I attended Sandpiper Elementary School. We returned to Singapore in 2012, where I completed my secondary education at St. Joseph's Institution. After graduation, I enlisted in the Singapore Armed Forces and served as a tank specialist in 48th Battalion Singapore Armored Regiment. I am now living in the city of Ann Arbor, Michigan pursuing my bachelor's degree under the Singapore Public Service Commision Scholarship for Engineering.

Some areas I am excited to explore while pursuing my engineering degree are computer vision, autonomous robotics and user-centered design. Aside from my studies, I enjoy programming small games and am learning web development. I also enjoy playing the piano and have a massive collection of vintage video game consoles and games (Game Boy, SNES, Dreamcast etc). I don't do too much on the physical side aside from jogging and core training, but I do occasionally play badminton and windsurf.
